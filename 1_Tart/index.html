Fresh Strawberry Tart

    TART

  • 80g unsalted butter
  • 45g powder sugar
  • pinch of salt
  • 30g egg
  • 20g ground almonds
  • 130g cake flour
  • 25g bread flour

    ALMOND CREAM

  • 50g unsalted butter
  • 50g sugar
  • 50g ground almonds
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp ~ Vanilla extract
  • 6g cake flour

    CUSTARD CREAM

  • 2 egg yolks
  • 40g sugar
  • 12g cake flour
  • 200ml milk vanilla beans
  • 100ml heavy cream
  • 8g sugar

    TOPPING

  • chocolate
  • strawberry
